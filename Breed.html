German Spitz

PRICE: ₹5,000-₹45,000

About: The German spitz is a very rare breed in North America. A breed club, the German Spitz Club of America, is seeking full AKC recognition for the breed. It can be difficult to find a puppy because there are few breeders in the United States and reputable breeders breed few litters a year. If you find a breeder, be prepared to get on a waiting list for a puppy. Though many experts will say that you can expect to pay under $1,000 for a German spitz pup, some breeders may charge upwards of a few thousand dollars per dog
